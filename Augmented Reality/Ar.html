Augmented Reality

An interactive AR experience designed to blend Qatari culture with modern technology. Built by Mahdi Hasan for competition showcase.

What is this project?

This AR experience brings heritage landmarks and cultural icons to life through interactive 3D models. It allows users to explore Qatar’s traditions in a modern, immersive way.

Objective: To show how technology can preserve and celebrate culture using augmented reality.

Blending Culture with Technology

Our AR system overlays cultural artifacts in real-world space, merging history with innovation. Using 3D visualization and wireless interaction, users can activate different cultural models through connected physical buttons — bridging tangible experience with digital storytelling.

AR Models

The project includes three core models that symbolize Qatar’s culture and environment:

Desert Rose

Symbol of Qatar’s natural beauty and uniqueness. The AR model shows detailed crystalline texture and organic shape.

Bedouin Tent

Represents traditional life and hospitality. In AR, it can be explored from all sides as if standing inside the desert tent.

Al Zubarah Fort

One of Qatar’s UNESCO heritage sites, reconstructed digitally to showcase architecture and cultural preservation.

PixelBox

PixelBox is an 8×8 Light controller with Bluetooth interface. It has Two modes : Animation (sequence frames) and Realtime (live control from your phone).

What is this project?

PixelBox turns simple pixel drawings into moving light. Use your phone to draw pixels live or build animations from frames and play them on the physical panel.

How it works (quick)

  1. Phone app -> sends design.
  2. System receives design and displays it.
  3. Matrix displays the frame. Animation mode sequences frames; Realtime updates instantly.

Modes

Animation (demo)

Add frames, set FPS, preview and send to PixelBox to play the sequence. Watch full demo: Watch Animation on YouTube

Realtime (demo)

Draw on the phone — the matrix updates instantly. Adjustable brightness and presets supported. Watch full demo: Watch Realtime on YouTube

Why it counts as art

PixelBox is more than a gadget. It transforms data, motion, and pattern into an expressive medium of light — a fusion of technology and aesthetics. Each pixel sequence behaves like a brushstroke of digital color, forming shifting geometric rhythms and cultural motifs. Through motion and luminosity, static pixel art evolves into kinetic light compositions — a small but living form of art.
